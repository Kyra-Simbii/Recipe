Crispy Honey Glazed Tofu

Vegan

Vegeterian

Gluten Free

This Crispy Tofu is going to be your new favorite recipe! Tossed with cornstarch and tasty spices, this meal is packed full of flavor!

Preparation

  • Prep Time: 1 hour
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes
  • Servings: 2 People

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce block Extra Firm Tofu
  • 1 tablespoon Cornstarch + 1 teaspoon
  • ¼ teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • ¼ teaspoon Onion Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Canola Oil
  • ⅓ cup Minced Shallot
  • 1 tablespoon Minced Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Water
  • ¼ cup Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • Honey drizzle
  • Chopped Chives or Sliced Green Onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Start by draining the block of tofu
  2. Slice tofu in half, so that you have two thinner slices of tofu
  3. Wrap tofu slices in a kitchen towel and press with a heavy object for 30 minutes - 1 hour
  4. Once pressed, slice into roughly 16 1-inch cubes
  5. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of cornstarch over the cubes, making sure to coat each side of the tofu cubes
  6. Season the cubes with onion powder and garlic powder
  7. Heat the stainless steel pan
  8. Once the pan is hot, heat 2 tablespoon canola oil
  9. Place each cube in the oil. Let each side crisp for 3-5 minutes on each side.
  10. Remove cubes once crispy and place on a paper towel to drain them
  11. In the same pan, add your minced shallots and garlic.
  12. Sautee the shallot and garlic until translucent and just slightly crispy
  13. In a separate bowl or measuring cup, mix 1 teaspoon of cornstarch with 1 tablespoon of water and mix until there are no clumps remaining
  14. Add ¼ cup of soy sauce to the pan
  15. Add the cornstarch slurry and mix. Once this reaches a boiling point, it will become thick.
  16. Once the sauce is thick, add your tofu to your serving dish and pour the sauce over the top.
  17. Drizzle honey over the top or include it in the sauce. Use as much or as little as you like.
  18. Garnish with sliced green onions, chopped chives, or cilantro.
